About Us

Our Party Name

 We chose our party name to be intentionally vague. When you hear, “Canadian Liberal Party” or “Canadian Conservative Party”, you immediately have a pretty good idea of the politics this party runs on. If you don’t agree ideologically with your conception of the party, you will not want to learn any more about them. When you hear, “The Canadian Action Party,” you are intrigued. A name like ours forces someone to do some research into the party, where they can form a full (and hopefully positive) opinion. By having a name that does not directly betray our politics, we hope to generate interest from all over the political spectrum.

Our Party Logo

 Our logo is meant to be simple and bold. We include the Maple leaf, as all parties do, and as a symbol of national pride. It should be memorable, and easy enough to draw. We have the word “ACT” bolded in the centre of our logo. This word easily identifies our political party, while sticking with the generally dynamic and minimal theme of our logo. We chose this word instead of an initialism for our party, because we believe it is more recognizable and symbolic. It also serves as a reminder of what our party promises to do, with immediacy.

Our Party Slogan

 Our slogan is “Progress with Purpose.” A common criticism of left-leaning parties like ours is the pointless changing of working institutions and traditions that need no changing. We have chosen a slogan that fits our bi-partisan-appealing theme, where we display our committment to progress, but also promise to keep it reasonable and fair to everyone.
